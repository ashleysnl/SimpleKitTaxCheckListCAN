FAQ

Common questions about this tax checklist

This planner is meant to organize your tax prep workflow. It does not calculate taxes or replace professional advice.

Is this a tax calculator?

No. This tool is a customizable organizer for receipts, slips, contacts, and filing tasks. It helps you prepare, not calculate taxes owed.

Can I reuse the checklist next year?

Yes. Save your current planner as a checklist file, then load it later. You can also clear all checkmarks while keeping your custom categories, notes, and contact details.

What can I track inside each item?

Each item supports a title, checked state, notes, contact name, contact email, contact phone, tags, and nested sub-items for follow-up steps.

What if my browser storage gets reset?

Your active planner auto-saves in localStorage for convenience, but saving a checklist file is the best backup if you want something portable outside the browser.
